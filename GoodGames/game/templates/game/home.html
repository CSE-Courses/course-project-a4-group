What is GoodGames?

GoodGames is a platform for gamers and friends to come together to play in tournaments and game matches. You can join tournaments or you can create your own or you can play against another gamer. After you create an account, you can begin earning different kinds of currency depending on what you are participating in.

About Currency

  • Public Tournaments

    When you participate in GoodGames sponsored public tournaments, which are open to all registered users of GoodGames, you can earn GG currency which can be redeemed at the GG Shop.

  • Private Tournaments

    When you participate private tournaments with friends on GoodGames, you can earn custom currency which you and your friends can decide what the real world value is.

  • Private Game Matches

    When you play private matches against another gamer or friend, you can choose to wager custom currency and decide what the real world value is.

Navigation

Once you login, on the left sidebar you will be able to view your dashboard, your private tournaments, your profile, your detailed game statistics, and the GG shop. You will also be able to submit private game match results against another person and have it recorded to your account.
